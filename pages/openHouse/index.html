Game 1: Peacemakers

My Critique for Peacemakers

For a first-launch game, it shows good promise and I feel like the developers can easily expand more on what they have currently and work on even more features later on. My only qualm about the game is its wonky animations and how the enemies' attacks are not telegraphed, making it difficult to judge how and when they initiate.

Game 2: Human 'N Flame

My Critique for Human N' Flame

This game definitely has alot of promise. Some tweaks could be done with gameplay but other than that, most of the animations for each character were well-made. The bugs I discovered in this game were photographed and are shown above.

Bugs Discovered

About the bugs...

Between the games, Peacemakers definitely had lesser bugs since it has alot less to offer compared to Human N' Flame. The bugs have been verified and checked by the developers themselves. I was very keen with bug-testing since I have experience beta-testing for videogames.
